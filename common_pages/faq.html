What People Frequently Ask?

What browser is preferred for HackerRocks skill-tests?

We recommend to use the latest stable versions of Google Chrome (version 40 or above) and Mozilla Firefox (version 35 or above) for a best experience.

What is General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance?

GDPR is the recent Privacy Protection Regulation introduced on April 27th, 2016 by the European Union (EU) to replace its previous Data Protection Regime. GDPR was launched to safeguard personal / professional information of EU citizens. Irrespective of the geographic presence, GDPR applies to all relevant companies or individuals who deal with the personal data of EU citizens. This section educates how GDPR can influence participant's activities on the HackerRocks offerings.

For what duration candidate's / participant's information be stored?

All the information / data will be intact until a candidate requests it to be deleted or deleted by the company that organized the skill-test.

What all information does HackerRocks collects from candidates?

HackerRocks securely collects the most common details from the recruiters. Below fields are selected by default:
  • Name
  • e-Mail ID
  • Location (City / State / Country)
  • Resume
  • University / College
  • Graduation Year
  • Additional Certification (if any)
Skill-Test administrators, however, can add custom fields based on their requirements.

Is all the personal data on HackerRocks is protected from third-party services?

HackerRocks securely stores all the personal information of candidates and do not share, rent, or sell this data to any third parties without the consent of skill-test organizers.
HackerRocks do, however, share Personal Data within Brainmeasures and its affiliated businesses, business partners, hired service vendors, and / or authorized third-party contractors for data processing or storage purposes, as well as to provide requested services, including:
  • Websites Hosting
  • Event and Seminar Hosting and Registrations
  • Customer Support

Note: The purpose of sharing Personal Data to these third parties is purely to complete / utilize the requested services or transaction.

How to get personal information deleted / updated on HackerRocks and how long will it take to process such request?

Candidates need to contact the skill-test administrators to get their personal details deleted / updated from being processed. Such requests, however, will get processed in a minimum of 30 days from the date when request is received.

Can the deleted information be restored?

No, once the data is deleted it cannot be restored.

Is it possible to delete the recorded snapshots / videos that the HackerRocks' system captures during the test?

Presently, there video / snapshot deletion capability is not available for candidates, however, they can request via email to the skill-test organizers, who further sends the request to the HackerRocks team to delete the recorded visuals. The whole process will take about 30 days from the date when request is received.

What is HackerRocks' role regarding candidate's personal data?

HackerRocks is essentially a skill-testing platform for organizations looking to hire talent as per their requirements. Skill-tests, however, are owned and administered by the organizers or the company.
Moreover, the responsibility of updating and deleting candidates' personal data on their request also lies with the company, while HackerRocks just act as a facilitator to process such requests.

HackerRocks is a platform for amateur and professional individuals to get trained on advanced technologies and other multi-disciplinary online courses, while also allow them to practice their coding and interview skills. Additionally, HackerRocks provides a pedestal for programmers from all over the world to come together and solve coding problems for advanced technologies, like machine learning, artificial intelligence, and more.

Does HackerRocks provide an opportunity to make money or get a job?

Yes! You can win grand cash prizes by participating in our challenges / hackathons or showcase your programming potential and get easily noticed by the global brands.

What kind of Challenges does HackerRocks list on its portal?

How can I test and submit my code?

When you are through with your code, click Run Code button to test if your code is running as desired. How can I debug my code?

Can I Practice codes online before participating in any challenge?

Yes, we have practice on over 3000+ coding languages and specialization on HackerRocks.

How can I practice Mock Interviews?

There is a section on HackerRocks website that enables candidates like you to practice Mock Interviews. You can choose from 2500+ practice interviews categories under skills and companies. Make your choice and practice to be easily crack interviews.

How to create new skill-tests after logging into the HackerRocks Admin Panel?

Click Create New Test button on the HackerRocks Admin Panel to develop new skill tests

Can participant view the sections created through HackerRocks Admin Panel?

No, sections that are created on HackerRocks Admin Panel are not visible to candidates as they can be accessed and modified by the recruiters / administrators only.

Is it possible to set time-restriction for answering MCQs?

Yes, time-restrictions to answer MCQs can be enabled / customized.

Is there any limit to the count of questions for a skill-test?

No, there is no such limit and recruiters can add as many questions as they want for their skill-testing requirements.

What is the required size dimension to upload an image during registration?

There is no size dimensions defined for uploading an image. We, however, recommend that you do not upload large sized images.

What are type of skill-tests are available on HackerRocks?

We have Auto Proctoring Tests, Human Proctoring Tests, and Customized Tests options for Enterprises.

General Public also access and attempt HackerRocks Skill-Tests?

No HackerRocks skill-tests are specifically designed to meet the requirements of Enterprises and only "invited" candidates, which are selected by the Enterprises themselves, can attempt or access the tests.

How to troubleshoot "Compatibility Check Fail" error, even after setting the camera permissions to "Allowed"?

Here are quick steps to pass the Compatibility Check:
  • Make sure the camera is not being used in multiple tabs or browsers.
  • If it is open in multiple browsers, then close the tabs (or browsers) and "hard refresh" (Ctrl+F5) the test page.
  • System shall now Pass the Compatibility Check for the Webcam.
  • If this still doesn't work then:
    • Close and re-open the browser
    • Clear all cookies and re-open the skill-test in a new browser

For how long does HackerRocks can store candidate's data?

Candidate's information is stored on HackerRocks servers until it is deleted, while for deleting any information, organizations need to send an email request to HackerRocks team on Info@HackerRocks.com.

Who among HackerRocks team can access candidate's data?

Candidates' data that is lying on HackerRocks is confidential and nobody even from the HackerRocks team has direct access to candidate's personal information.
The only reason that HackerRocks agent shall temporarily access candidate's information is to understand the concern for a raised support request.

Who can access candidate's data from organization that organized the skill-test?

Primarily the administrators who have access to the candidate's skill-test reports can view candidate's information as well.

How candidates can stop their data to process?

HackerRocks platform automatically processes the data while retaining it. The only way, however, candidates can stop their data from processing is by requesting the skill-test administrator to completely delete the data.

How a skill-test administrator can request to update or delete a candidates' data?

Administrators need to send an email at Info@HackerRocks.com along with the list of candidates whose data needs to be updated or deleted. Organization can also route such requests through their designated Account Managers
HackerRocks shall remove the data from server within 30 days of receiving the request. HackerRocks shall send a confirmation email post the data is updated or deleted.

What all audit logs does HackerRocks maintains and how can these logs be obtained / accessed?

HackerRocks maintains audit logs of the company's skill-test administrators as well as the activity logs of candidates who participate for the test.
Companies or recruiters can request for these audit logs by sending an email to info@HackerRocks.com or through their designated Account Manager.
